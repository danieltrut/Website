ABOUT ME - ABOUT ME - ABOUT ME - ABOUT ME

Junior fullstack developer & UX / UI Designer

I’m Daniel, a software developer living in Tartu, Estonia. My coding journey began in the end of 2018. I study Web Development in Tartu Vocational Education Center, Estonia (autumn 2020 – 2022). Have a serious interest for UI effects and Development. Worked two years as a Front-end developer & UX / UI Designer in digital agency "AVDAY". Some of the works where I took part in design received awards.

This only increased my appetite, and so in fall 2020 I decided to enroll in an advanced professional certificate program in web development at the Tartu Vocational Education Centre. In my free time I am working to improve my front-end and back-end skills. Love that now I can combine Design and Web Development. I want to develop my back-end skills. Thats why I want to find place for internship.
